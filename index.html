About Me

My main interest in the field of library and information science is improving user access to information, on the Web or otherwise. I am specifically interested in the development, management and analysis of structured and unstructured data and metadata. I am also interested in communicating technical knowledge to both technical and non-technical audiences. In addition to my information science background, I hold both Bachelor's and Master's degrees in voice and opera and have sung opera, oratorio and chamber music professionally for 12 years.
